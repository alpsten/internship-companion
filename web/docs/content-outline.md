# Content Outline

This document maps the guided plan to supporting resources.

## Prepare

Goal:

- Help students present themselves clearly and professionally.

Typical support:

- LinkedIn guide
- CV guidance
- pitch examples
- portfolio and GitHub advice

## Network

Goal:

- Help students find warm contacts and ask for introductions.

Typical support:

- networking guide
- outreach preparation notes
- examples of short networking messages

## Outreach

Goal:

- Help students contact companies with confidence and specificity.

Typical support:

- email templates
- LinkedIn templates
- call script
- follow-up examples

## Interviews

Goal:

- Help students prepare stories, questions, and reflections.

Typical support:

- interview guide
- STAR examples
- post-interview reflection prompts

## Momentum

Goal:

- Help students keep going, review progress, and stay organized.

Typical support:

- application log
- progress log
- weekly review
- celebration and motivation prompts

## Resource Rules

Every resource should:

- solve one clear problem
- fit at least one plan stage
- be usable within minutes
- include a practical next step
- avoid unnecessary fluff

## File Types

- `Guide`: teaching and orientation
- `Mall`: reusable message or structure
- `Tracker`: downloadable support for repeated use
