# Product Brief

## Product

Internship Companion is a guided companion for LIA and internship students.

The product should help students:

- break the search into manageable steps
- know what to do next
- access the right template, guide, or tracker at the right moment

## Core Positioning

The plan is the primary product experience.

Resources support the plan. They are not the whole product on their own.

That means the product should feel like:

- a calm guide first
- a resource library second

## Audience

Primary audience:

- Swedish LIA and internship students in tech and adjacent digital fields

Secondary audience:

- students who need English-language support
- contributors improving the content and experience

## User Promise

Within a few minutes, a student should be able to:

- understand where they are in the process
- choose a next action
- find a practical resource that helps them act

## Core Journey

1. Prepare profile, portfolio, CV, LinkedIn, and pitch.
2. Build a warm network.
3. Reach out with personalized contact.
4. Prepare for interviews.
5. Maintain momentum and track progress.

## Product Principles

- Be concrete, not vague.
- Be supportive, not corporate.
- Prefer next actions over long theory.
- Keep resources practical and easy to use immediately.
- Avoid stale copy and hardcoded dates unless required.

## Success Signals

- Students keep returning to the plan flow.
- Students open supporting resources from within the plan.
- Downloadable trackers and templates are used.
- The product feels clear and trustworthy.
