## Why celebrate?
Your brain needs rewards to keep going. Marking every step—even the small ones—builds momentum.

## Quick rewards
- Share a "win" message in the class chat.
- Take a walk or podcast break after an outreach session.
- Give yourself 30 minutes on a passion project.

## Bigger milestones
- Treat yourself to coffee with a friend after the first interview.
- Upgrade a piece of your study setup when you get a "yes" or valuable feedback.

## Track your wins
- Add a column "What I celebrated" in your application log.
- Capture a "win of the week" in your weekly reflection.

## Remember
Celebrate the actions you control: making a call, sending a thoughtful follow-up, learning something new. It builds resilience.
