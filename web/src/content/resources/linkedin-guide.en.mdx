## Why LinkedIn?
It is the first place many supervisors look. A sharp profile tells them you take your LIA search seriously.

## Quick checklist
- **Profile photo:** professional lighting, no selfies.
- **Headline:** for example `Java Developer Student | Internship (LIA) Spring 2025`.
- **About section:** who you are, what you study, what you are looking for.
- **Experience & education:** short bullet points with tech stacks.
- **Featured:** link to GitHub, portfolio or school projects.

## Tips
1. Switch to English if you target international teams; otherwise keep it consistent in Swedish.
2. Ask classmates for recommendations—fast social proof.
3. Comment on posts from companies you like. You stay visible even before you reach out.

## Next steps
- Update your profile and send it to a friend for quick feedback.
- Add your LinkedIn link to every email signature before you start contacting companies.
