## What the sheet helps you do
- Compare multiple companies without mixing them up.
- Write down why each company is a real fit for you.
- Track contact channel, next step, and follow-up timing.
- Prioritise which companies are worth your best energy right now.

## The columns cover
- Priority, company, location, and website
- Team focus and relevant tech stack
- Why the company matters to you
- Decision maker, contact person, and outreach channel
- Whether you have a warm contact and how you plan to apply
- Status, latest action, next step, and interview stage

## How to use it
1. Pick three to five companies.
2. Fill out the research before you send anything.
3. Write at least one concrete reason for each company.
4. Add the next step immediately after every contact.
5. Tag priority so you know which companies deserve your best attention first.

## Good rule
If you cannot fill in “Why this company?” with something specific, you probably are not ready to send the message yet.
