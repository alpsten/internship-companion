## Prepare your stories
Use the STAR method for two or three situations:
1. **Situation** – what happened?
2. **Task** – what were you responsible for?
3. **Action** – what did you do?
4. **Result** – what changed or what did you learn?

## Questions to rehearse
- Tell me about yourself.
- Why do you want to do your internship with us?
- What technical challenge have you solved recently?
- How do you handle not knowing something immediately?

## Questions you can ask
```
What does a typical day look like for an intern here?
Which languages/frameworks would I work with?
How do you support interns in their learning?
```

## After every interview
- Write down two things that went well and one thing to improve.
- Send a thank-you within 24 hours and repeat one highlight from the conversation.
- Update your application log with the status and next step.
