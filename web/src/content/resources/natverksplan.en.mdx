## Start with your map
- List everyone who might know companies: family, friends, classmates, teachers, former colleagues.
- For each person, note what they do and which companies they know.
- Tag *warm* contacts (you can reach out directly) and *introductions needed* (you’ll ask for a bridge).

## Ask for help the smart way
```
Hi [name]! I'm looking for a LIA internship in spring 2025 and wondered if you know any companies in [area]. Is there someone you think I should talk to?
```
- Be clear about timing and what type of internship you want.
- Include your quick pitch or LinkedIn link so they can forward it in seconds.

## Track every thread
- Add each contact to your application log and mark it as "network".
- Note the last time you reached out and when to follow up.
- Always say thank you—even if it doesn’t turn into something right away.

## Bonus moves
- Post a short update on LinkedIn every other week: "Spoke with X companies—still searching for a LIA!" It makes it easy for others to help you.
