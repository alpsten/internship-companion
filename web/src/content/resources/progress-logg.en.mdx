## How to use it
- Every day, add a row with the date, what you did, and what comes next.
- Tag the activity as "reach out", "follow up", or "learning".
- Add a quick mood rating (⭐⭐⭐) to spot when you need rest.

## Columns
1. Date
2. Activity
3. Type (reach out / follow up / learning)
4. Outcome
5. Next step + date
6. Mood / energy

## Tips
- Keep the log open while you work—updating it takes 10 seconds.
- Build a filter view for all follow-ups you need to send next week.

## Bonus
- Tie the log to your weekly reflection and summarise what drove progress.
- Share a snippet with a mentor or teacher if you want feedback on your search rhythm.
