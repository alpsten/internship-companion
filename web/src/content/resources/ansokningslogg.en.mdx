## What to log
- Company name and contact person
- How you reached out (email, LinkedIn, call, in-person)
- Dates and status (sent, replied, interview, no, follow-up)
- Next action + reminder date

## How to use it
1. Set a weekly target, e.g. five new contacts.
2. Update the tracker right after every action.
3. Write the next step immediately (for example “Follow up 7 days later”).

## Formats
- **Google Sheets template** for quick duplication and filtering.
- **Printable checklist** if you like to highlight progress on paper.

## Bonus ideas
- Flag companies where you have a warm introduction.
- Add a column “What worked well” to spot patterns after a couple of weeks.
